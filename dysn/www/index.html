Basic Workshop

Start out by learning about 3D modeling and printing basics and combine them to create your own awesome 3D rocket. During the workshop you will design a 3D rocket which you will print to take home with you.

Overview

Introduction to 3D modeling & 3D printing.

Outcomes

  • Learn basic 3D modeling commnads and editing tools.
  • Export 3D models for 3D printing.
  • Setup a design to export and print your creation.

General

  • Prerequisites: Basic computer knowledge.
  • Duration: 1 day.
  • Ages: 10+

Advanced Workshop

The participants are facilitated in designing a small rocket-car propelled by an inflated balloon. The workshop consists of 4 X 90min sessions on a weekly basis. We take an in depth look at optimizing modeling tools and printing cost efficient parts to create and assemble a rocket car.

Overview

Introduction to 3D modeling & 3D printing.

Outcomes

  • Proficiency with 3D modeling application.
  • Combining editing and 3D modeling tools to achieve intricate designs.
  • 3D modeling for assembly.
  • Proficiency with operating a 3D printer.
  • Setting up design for cost effective printing.
  • Assembling 3D printing components

General Information

  • Prerequisites: Basic workshop(Rocket badge)
  • Duration: 4 sessions of 90 min.
  • Ages: 10+
